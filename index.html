Personal Project

Pinboard Refresh

I wanted a way to see my saved pins right on my laptop homescreen, refreshing automatically every day or so — so I built this.

Privacy Policy

What it does

Pretty simple, really — it pulls in my saved pins and displays them on my laptop homescreen. They refresh about once a day so I always have fresh visual inspiration without having to go dig for it.

Who it's for

Just me! This is a personal project I made for my own use. There's no public sign-up or anything like that — it's a single-user app that I built to scratch my own itch.
