Pinboard Refresh

A lightweight personal app that displays saved pins on a laptop homescreen and refreshes them automatically every day or so.

Privacy Policy

What the app does

  • Displays saved pins on a laptop homescreen view
  • Refreshes pin content automatically about once per day
  • Helps keep visual inspiration current without manual updates

App details

App name: Pinboard Refresh

Project type: Personal app

Primary user: Personal use (just one user)

Audience: Individual / personal account use
