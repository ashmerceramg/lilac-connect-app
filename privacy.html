Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 25, 2026

Lilac Wren App is a personal project. It reads my saved pins from Pinterest so I can display them on my laptop homescreen. I'm the only person who uses it.

The app only reads pin data — it doesn't post, edit, or delete anything. No data is shared, sold, or used for tracking or advertising. Pin data is cached locally and refreshed about once a day.

Questions? Reach me at pinboardrefresh.app@gmail.com.
