Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 25, 2026

1. What this app is

Pinboard Refresh is a personal app I built for myself. It connects to Pinterest to display my saved pins on my laptop homescreen and refreshes them about once a day. Nobody else uses it — it's just me.

2. What data the app accesses

  • My Pinterest account info needed to log in
  • My saved pins so they can be displayed on my homescreen

That's it. The app only reads pin data — it doesn't post, edit, or delete anything on Pinterest.

3. How the data is used

The app uses my pin data for one thing: showing my saved pins on my homescreen. There's no analytics, no tracking, no advertising — none of that.

4. Data sharing

I don't share or sell any data. The app talks to the Pinterest API to fetch my pins, and that's the only external service involved.

5. Data storage

Pin data is cached locally so the homescreen can load quickly. It gets refreshed roughly once a day and old data is replaced.

6. Security

Access tokens are stored securely and the app runs locally on my machine. Since I'm the only user, the attack surface is pretty minimal.

7. Contact

If you have questions for some reason, you can reach me at pinboardrefresh.app@gmail.com.
